--- layout: home author_profile: true title: "Welcome to the SLP Lab" --- ## About the Lab The **Speech and Language Processing (SLP) Lab** at the University of Nevada, Reno investigates human speech, linguistic structure, and multilingual language processing using computational and experimental methods. Our research lies at the intersection of linguistics, cognitive science, and natural language processing, with a focus on how humans acquire, represent, and process language across different modalities and linguistic backgrounds. ## Director **Santiago Arróniz, Ph.D.** leads the SLP Lab. He is a faculty member in the [Department of World Languages & Literatures](https://www.unr.edu/world-languages) at the University of Nevada, Reno. ## Affiliation The SLP Lab is affiliated with the **World Languages & Literatures Department** at the [University of Nevada, Reno](https://www.unr.edu).
